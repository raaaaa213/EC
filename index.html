Why We Built
StudyPlanner

Over 72% of high school students report feeling overwhelmed by exam prep and unorganised routines. StudyPlanner was created to help students:

  • Build better study habits and routines
  • Track moods and reflect on progress daily
  • Reduce stress and avoid burnout
  • Stay organised with tasks and deadlines
  • Gain insights from their own academic data

Our mission is to empower every student to take control of their learning journey and achieve their best, both academically and emotionally.
